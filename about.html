About Me

The i In My Name Is For interdisciplinary

Hi! I'm Brinda, an aspiring researcher and UX engineer. Trained in psychology with a focus on neuro and cognitive science, I'm focused on gaining interdisciplinary skills and knowledge that will allow me to leverage principles of human behaviour to design and deploy studies that can lay the groundwork for more intuitive, human-friendly devices and systems.

Apart from this, I enjoy teaching myself code and fiddling around with web development. I'm currently learning HTML/CSS and am hoping to start learning Javascript soon as I prepare for a career in UX research and engineering

When I'm not crying over code, I enjoy reading science fiction and magical realism novels while running through my stash of tea at an alarming pace.
